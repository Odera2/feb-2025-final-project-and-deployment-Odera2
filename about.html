About Stanly Odera

Hello! I'm Stanly Odera, a passionate web developer and designer based in Nairobi, Kenya. My work revolves around building interactive, accessible, and beautiful web experiences.

I created this blog to document my learning, share insights, and help others in the tech community grow. Expect posts on JavaScript, design thinking, life as a developer, and everything in between.

Outside of coding, I enjoy having serene nature walks, socialising with friends, and also farming. These activities keep me grounded and inspire creativity in my work.
